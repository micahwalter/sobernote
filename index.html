2016 MAR. 26th: An update is long overdue, as the last one was...2 years ago! (Giving new meaning to 'leap year'...) Yes, 2015 came and went, but not without progress. In addition, I've been putting together a group of good musicians to assist in the dispensing of notes - hence the name "Sober and the Notes." We'll be hitting the rehearsal studio regularly and hope to do a number of dates over the next few months, starting late spring, most likely! Drop by again for show info and other pertinent news!
